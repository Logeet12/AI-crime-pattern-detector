AI Crime-Pattern Detector

Our AI Crime Pattern Detector uses synthetic data generation, preprocessing, and constraint-based logic (CSP) to analyze suspects. It evaluates parameters like motive strength, alibi, evidence, distance, and witness reports to identify potential high-risk individuals and uncover crime patterns. We don't eliminate suspects with low score but reduce the chance of them being the culprit.
